Gorillas

The mountain gorilla is a specific type and means what it sounds like. They live in the african mountains. These gorillas are omnivores which means they can eat plants and animals. The mountain gorilla can live up to 35 years and weighs up to almost 500 pounds. They live were they can chill out but surprisingly these animals are mainly peaceful. They spend a lot of time eating mainly plants but some insects too. These gorillas live where they can have there own green space and keep there family safe.
