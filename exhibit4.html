llamas

Llamas are an interesting animal for many reasons. Llamas can be protective caring and sometimes just flat out mean. Llamas are pets a lot of the time but they can live in lots of different places around the world. mostly in the cold but all llamas need to survive is just food and water. A llama can use its own spit to defend its self and to get hit by it would hurt, but they can also bite and llamas are cool but only go near one if you know it is safe.
