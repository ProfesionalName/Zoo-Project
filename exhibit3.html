Bears

The Brown bear is a very interesting bear for many reasons. One reason being that they hibernate in the winter time. The brown bear is a omnivore and can eat plants and animals. The brown bear lives in mountain/woods area. They can get up to 8 feet tall and weight up to 700 pounds. The brown bear eats a lot of food and they especially eat a lot of fish. This bear has a den which is like a house slightly under ground. They live there lives there that is even where they hibernate. They have to eat so much to prepare there bodys to go to sleep for that long. All in all these are some cool bears and are fun to learn about.
